David E. Goldberg is a member of the firm’s Corporate/Mergers and Acquisitions practice group.

Experiences
Advise clients in a variety of strategic and operational matters, including corporate formation, choice of entity, mergers and acquisitions, joint ventures and leasing projects
Draft, review and negotiate material project documents, including engineering, procurement and construction contracts and operation, maintenance and power purchase agreements, for various alternative power producing technologies, including solar, nuclear and wind facilities
Draft, review and negotiate transaction documents for a variety of asset and stock purchase transactions, including purchase, employment, non-compete, consulting and escrow agreements, and related corporate organizational and governance documents
Represent financial institutions and borrowers in all aspects of debt financings and facilities, including credit, loan, security and subordination agreements
Represent hotel owners and operators in all aspects of real estate acquisition and development projects, including limited partnership, operating, management, franchise and easement agreements, leases and corporate governance documents

Represent public real estate investment trusts and developers in the acquisition, financing, development, management and leasing of improved and unimproved real property
