About Me

By now you should realize that my name is Alex Beutel - I am a fifth year Ph.D. candidate in computer science at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Professor Christos Faloutsos. I research large-scale user behavior modeling, covering fraud detection, recommendation systems, and scalable machine learning.

Before being a graduate student, I studied computer science and physics at Duke University. At Duke I researched computational geometry with Professor Pankaj Agarwal and Thomas Mølhave, ran the Duke Schedulator, and was the Director of Online Development for The Chronicle.
